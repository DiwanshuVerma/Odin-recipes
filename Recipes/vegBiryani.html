One pot easy Vegetable Biryani

This easy vegetable biryani is a meatless Indian delicacy perfect for vegetarian or vegan folks out there.

How Is This Veg Biryani Healthy?

  • It is prepared using different fresh vegetables like cauliflower, carrot, french beans, green peas. Therefore, it contains nutrients from all these fresh vegetables.
  • It does not contain added clarified butter (aka ghee). Therefore, it is low in fat content.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup basmati riced
  • 1 cup green peasd
  • 2 pieces medium carrots diced into 1" to 1 ½"d
  • 2 cup cauliflower florets (around 1″ size)d
  • 1 cup green beansd
  • 2 large onions slicedd
  • ½ cup curdd
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juiced
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh mintd
  • ⅓ cup chopped fresh cilantrod
  • 2-3 green chilliesd
  • 2-3 cloves garlic mincedd
  • 1 teaspoon black cumin seedsd
  • 2 tablespoon biryani masala (or more if needed)d
  • 1 tablespoon avocado oil or any other cooking oild
  • 4 cup water or vegetable stockd
  • salt to tasted

Instructions

  1. Soak rice in water and set aside for 15-20 min.
  2. Heat oil in a large heavy bottom pot on medium to high heat. Add onions and saute them until caramelize (for about 8-10 min).
  3. Put black cumin, minced garlic, green chili and cook for about 1 min
  4. Add cauliflower, carrots and french beans and saute for 6-8 min
  5. Finally add yogurt, lemon juice, green peas and soaked rice: gently mix well.
  6. Season the mixture with salt and biryani masala.
  7. Add water or vegetable stock and bring mixture to boil.
  8. Then add freshly chopped mint and cilantro and simmer the biryani mixture on low heat for about 18-20 min.
  9. Serve warm to enjoy this flavorful veg biryani.
